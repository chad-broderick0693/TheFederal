About The Federal

It is the sole aim of the Federal Coffee House to proudly provide its community with ethically sourced and locally-roasted coffee, as well as a public space for the community to gather and share

Coffee houses played an important role in the American Revolution and the founding of the United States. The Federal Coffee House of Mowrystown, Ohio continues this lineage of providing an open space for the public to gather and share in one another's interests and day-to-day, all in the Federal Era style and decor of the time period. After the Revolutionary War, patriots were paid for their service by a limited federal government in grants of land scattered through the territory now known as the Midwest. Knowing this, the Federal Coffee House proudly serves fresh cups of ethically-sourced, locally-roasted coffee to our own and surrounding communities in a small portion of those granted lands in southwestern Ohio
